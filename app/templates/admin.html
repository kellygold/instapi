InstaPi Guide

📷 Upload Photos

The easiest way to add photos. Tap "Upload Photos" to pick images from your camera roll. Works from any device — iPhone, Android, or computer. Share the upload link with family so they can add photos too.

➕ Google Photos Picker

Select photos directly from your Google Photos library. Requires signing in with Google each time. Best for picking specific photos you've already organized in Google Photos.

🖼️ Photo Gallery

View all photos on your frame. Tap the × on any photo to delete it. Use the Small/Large buttons to change the grid size. On USB mode, remember to hit "Sync Frame" after deleting photos.

🔄 Sync Frame (USB mode)

When you delete photos, the USB frame doesn't update immediately. An orange "Sync Frame" button appears — tap it to push your changes to the frame. The frame will briefly disconnect and reconnect.

🎬 Slideshow Settings

Slide Duration: How long each photo is shown (3–60 seconds).
Transition: How photos change — fade, slide, or zoom.
Shuffle: Random order instead of sequential.
Ken Burns: A gentle slow pan and zoom effect on each photo.

In HDMI mode, these control the actual slideshow. In USB mode, they only affect the browser preview — your frame uses its own built-in slideshow.

↩️ Factory Reset

Deletes all photos and returns the frame to the setup screen. On USB mode, the frame will show the QR code again. This cannot be undone.

⚙️ System

Update & Restart: Pulls the latest code from GitHub and restarts the app.
Switch Mode: Toggle between USB and HDMI display modes. Requires a restart to take full effect.
